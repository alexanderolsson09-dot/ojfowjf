About

Alexander Olsson is a Swedish competitive sailor with a clear high-performance mindset. His campaign is built on structure, discipline, technical precision, and steady progression toward long-term international success.

Training Philosophy

Daily work combines on-water execution, physical preparation, and tactical review. Every block is designed to strengthen consistency in starts, speed, and decision-making.

Long-Term Ambition

The objective is sustained elite development: competing in stronger international fleets, refining race performance, and building a durable pathway to top-level sailing.
