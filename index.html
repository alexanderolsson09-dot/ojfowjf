About

Alexander Olsson is one of Sweden’s most accomplished junior sailors, competing internationally in the 29er class and representing KSSS – Royal Swedish Yacht Club. He competed in the National Team Junior and National Team Youth categories and belonged to Svenska Seglarförbundet during high school.

Elite Development Background

At age 16, Alexander moved away from home to attend Motala National Elite Sports High School (RIG), combining studies with elite sailing. His profile is built on resilience, coachability, and long-term dedication to high-performance sport.

Training & Competitive Commitment

Sailing has been central throughout high school, with roughly 25–30 hours per week of training and competition. Since age 14, he has trained and raced internationally in countries including Italy, Denmark, and Germany.

29er Class

The 29er is a high-performance two-person skiff that demands advanced boat handling, speed, physical endurance, and close coordination between helm and crew. It is one of the most competitive youth development classes in sailing and a strong pathway toward elite and Olympic-level performance.
